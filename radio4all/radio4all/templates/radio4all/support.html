The A-Infos Radio Project is an all-volunteer, non-profit service and we rely on the contributions of our users and supporters to continue operating. Below are a few of the best ways you can help keep the project going.

Cash Donations:

Monetary donations are always needed. You can send us tax-deductible donations (you decide how often and how much) via PayPal. Use the Donate buttons below or send to r4a@radio4all.net using the Paypal site. Either a one-time donation or an ongoing automatic donation subscription for $5 or $10 per month. If most people who used the Radio Project donated only a few dollars per year, we'd never have to worry about money again.

radio4all is now an activity (at the moment, the only activity) of Northern California Public Broadcasting, an IRS 501(c)(3) nonprofit that was formed in 1990.

Mirror Our Archive!

If you, your employer, an NGO or a University you are connected with can offer us someting in the neighborhood of two terabytes (2,000 GB) of storage space to store a mirror of our entire archive, and a decent amount of bandwith to serve the mirror, by all means get in touch with us at # . The more sites mirroring our archive, the better the access to our archive will be for people around the world.
